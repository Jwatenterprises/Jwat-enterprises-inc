One of the biggest myths in small business is that funding is only for established companies with perfect credit and years of tax returns. That's not true anymore.

ROK Financial has changed the game for small business owners by connecting businesses of every size, stage, and credit profile with the right funding — fast. Whether you're a startup looking for seed capital, a growing business that needs equipment, or an established company ready to scale, there's a program designed for you.

Here's a breakdown of everything ROK Financial offers and how to know which option fits your business right now.

What Is ROK Financial?

ROK Financial is a business funding marketplace that works with an extensive network of lenders to match business owners with the best loan options for their specific situation. Instead of applying to one bank and waiting weeks, ROK Financial puts your application in front of multiple lenders simultaneously — giving you more options, better rates, and faster approvals.

Key advantage: One application. Multiple lenders. Decisions in as little as 24 hours. No hard credit pull to check your options.

Loan Programs Available Through ROK Financial

Business Term Loans

Lump-sum funding repaid over a fixed term with predictable monthly payments. Best for expansion, hiring, or major investments.

Up to $5M  ·  1–5 year terms

Business Line of Credit

Revolving credit you draw from as needed and only pay interest on what you use. Ideal for managing cash flow and covering gaps.

Up to $250K  ·  Revolving access

SBA Loans

Government-backed loans with low interest rates and long repayment terms. Best for established businesses with strong documentation.

Up to $5M  ·  Lowest rates available

Equipment Financing

Fund any equipment purchase and use the equipment itself as collateral. No additional assets required.

Up to $5M  ·  100% equipment cost

Merchant Cash Advance

Funding based on your daily credit card or debit sales. Repayment is a percentage of daily revenue — no fixed payments.

Up to $500K  ·  Fast approval

Invoice Factoring

Turn unpaid invoices into immediate cash. Sell your outstanding invoices and get funded within 24 hours — no waiting on clients to pay.

Up to 90% of invoice value

Revenue-Based Financing

Funding based on your monthly revenue, not your credit score. Repay as a percentage of monthly revenue — flexible and scalable.

Up to $500K  ·  No fixed payments

Startup Business Loans

Funding for businesses with limited history. ROK Financial works with startups and early-stage businesses often turned away by traditional banks.

Available with 6+ months in business

What You Need to Apply

Requirements vary by program, but in general you'll need:

  • At least 3–6 months in business (some programs require 1 year)
  • Minimum $10,000–$15,000 in monthly revenue (varies by program)
  • Business bank statements (last 3–6 months)
  • Government-issued ID
  • EIN or Social Security Number

No tax returns required for many programs. No hard credit pull to check your options.

How Fast Can You Get Funded?

Speed is one of ROK Financial's biggest advantages over traditional lenders:

  • Merchant Cash Advance: As fast as 24 hours
  • Revenue-Based Financing: 24–48 hours
  • Business Term Loan: 2–5 business days
  • Line of Credit: 3–7 business days
  • SBA Loans: 30–90 days (more documentation required)
  • Equipment Financing: 2–5 business days
