The "Follow-Up Gap" and What It Costs You

If you don't have an automated follow-up system, you are likely suffering from the "Follow-Up Gap." This is the space between a lead showing interest and you actually engaging with them. Here is what happens in that gap:

  • Lead Decay: The probability of connecting with a lead drops by 10x after just the first hour.
  • Competitor Entry: Your lead is likely searching for solutions. If you don't answer, they’ll click the next result in Google.
  • Faded Interest: The emotional "high" of wanting to solve a problem fades quickly. If you don't strike while the iron is hot, you'll have to resell them from scratch later.
The JWAT Rule: A lead that isn't contacted within 5 minutes is a lead that is 80% more likely to buy from someone else. You can't do that manually, but your CRM can.
